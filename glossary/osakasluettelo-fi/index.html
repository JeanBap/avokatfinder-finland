Osakasluettelo

Osakasluettelo Finland – Housing Company Shareholder Register

The osakasluettelo (shareholder register) is the official record of asunto-osake (housing company share) ownership in Finland. Since 2019, the register for registered housing companies has been maintained by the National Land Survey of Finland (Maanmittauslaitos) as part of the national asset register, replacing the previous paper-based share certificate system.

When an asunto-osake is sold, the transfer is recorded in the osakasluettelo by the buyer's bank or broker notifying Maanmittauslaitos of the change. The buyer's ownership is officially established only when the osakasluettelo entry is updated — analogous to land register registration for real property. The entry update is typically made within a few days of closing.

The 2019 reform also abolished paper share certificates (osakekirja) for housing companies that have joined the electronic register. Buyers of asunto-osakkeet in registered companies receive no physical certificate — their ownership exists entirely as an electronic entry. This has eliminated certain fraud risks associated with forged share certificates.

For housing companies that have not yet joined the electronic system (typically older companies), paper osakekirjat remain in use. Buyers in these transactions take delivery of the physical share certificates as evidence of ownership. The transition to the electronic system is ongoing.

Mortgage lenders note their pant (lien) in the osakasluettelo rather than through separate pledge documentation. This simplified the security registration process and eliminated the need for physical share certificate endorsements in the electronic system.

⚠ Common MistakeDelaying the notification to Maanmittauslaitos after purchase. Until the osakasluettelo is updated, the buyer's title is not officially recorded, creating risk if the seller were to pledge the shares to a third party (though this is extremely rare).
💡 Expert TipConfirm with your bank that the osakasluettelo entry has been updated promptly after completion. Most Finnish banks handle this automatically as part of the mortgage registration process, but it is worth confirming within a week of closing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I check the osakasluettelo as a prospective buyer?

The seller can provide an extract from the osakasluettelo confirming their ownership. The register itself is not fully publicly accessible but the isännöitsijäntodistus references the registered owner.

What is the legal effect of registration in the osakasluettelo?

Registration creates an official record of title and priority against competing claims. A buyer registered in the osakasluettelo has strong protection against claims by third parties based on prior unregistered agreements.

How long does the osakasluettelo update take?

For companies in the electronic system, the update is typically processed within one to three business days of the notification being submitted.
